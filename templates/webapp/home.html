Engine Oil

We are indulged in offering to our patrons an exclusive range of Diesel Engine Oil. These oils are processed by industry known experts who have prosperous industry knowledge. The professionals are processed these oils keeping in mind the set industry norms. Our hygienic processing unit helps us in meeting the urgent requirements of our patrons.

Features

  • Hygienically packed
  • Longer shelf life
  • Protect from rust

description

It is suitable for all types of diesel passenger cars & commercial vehicles, earthmoving machinery, construction industry & power units for constant high speed or stop-start service under severe conditions. It is especially recommended for use in vehicles meeting EURO I & II norms.

Field Trials conducted on TELCO / Ashok Leyland vehicles established a drain interval of 24000 kms.


Performance Benefits:

  • Provides excellent lubrication The unique detergent-dispersant inhibitor used in this oil helps in keeping the engine clean, preventing premature oil thickening
  • Good TBN retention power thereby offering excellent protection against corrosive wear
  • This oil is suitable for use in all seasons and helps in reducing oil consumption

Performance Level:

  • API CF-4 / SJ
  • MB 228.1
  • IS 13656: 2002 Type EDL5 & EPL2
  • MILL-2104E
