Petrol Oil

Petrol, also known as gasoline, is a common and widely used fuel derived from crude oil through a refining process. It is a vital source of energy for internal combustion engines, primarily found in automobiles, motorcycles, and small power equipment. Petrol is known for its high energy density, making it an efficient and convenient fuel for transportation. However, its combustion produces carbon dioxide emissions, contributing to air pollution and climate change concerns. As the world seeks more sustainable energy sources, research and development in alternative fuels and electric vehicles continue to grow, aiming to reduce the environmental impact associated with petrol consumption.
